Who are we?

equiIT is a collection of engineers, who by pooling their resources, experience and expertise deliver top of the market services to companies, freelancers and individuals in need of tools, methods and final products in geoinfomatics, IT and surveying.

What do we do?

We are developers and engineers with years of experience and an expertise for handling spatial data.
We are well versed in software, database and web development.
We acquire spatial data from satellites, the web and the field.

How do we roll?

We like open source and enjoy the freedom it gives,
We channel the cummulative experience of the community
allowing us to speed the development process
and pass on the time and royalties savings to you.
We have a flexible organisation allowing us to be versitile
and work with anyone and in any timezone.
We are dedicated and highly motivated, mostly because we like what we do!
